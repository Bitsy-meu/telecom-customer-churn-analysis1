# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Strategy

## Project Overview

This project analyzes telecom customer churn using Tableau and SQL. The objective is to identify key drivers of customer attrition and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.

## Business Problem

Customer churn directly impacts revenue and business growth. Understanding why customers leave helps organizations develop targeted retention strategies and reduce customer loss.

## Dataset

* Telecom Customer Churn Dataset
* Total Customers: 7,043
* Churned Customers: 1,869
* Churn Rate: 26.5%

## Tools Used

* Tableau
* SQL
* GitHub

## Key Findings

### 1. Contract Type

Customers on month-to-month contracts exhibit significantly higher churn than customers on one-year or two-year contracts.

### 2. Payment Method

Customers using electronic checks show the highest churn rate among all payment methods.

### 3. Customer Tenure

Most churn occurs during the early months of the customer lifecycle. Long-term customers are substantially more likely to remain loyal.

## Recommendations

1. Encourage customers to move to longer-term contracts.
2. Improve onboarding during the first six months.
3. Investigate causes of churn among electronic-check customers.
4. Develop targeted retention campaigns for high-risk customer segments.
